WHAT IS OB MONTESSORI?


We are a progressive pre-k to 12 co-educational school, offering a viable alternative to international schools.

Our mission is to transform our students into self-developing and productive individuals who can succeed, direct, and influence the future of an ever-changing world.

Explore our programs and discover the vibrant community that makes OBMC a unique place for a child to grow and develop.
